Exponential growth of AI will make engineers role shifted. We no longer need weeks to write a feature, AI can just generate them in minutes. Engineers no longer just someone who writes codes. The situation expect us to understand the core value of the system, the business aspect of the feature, and the ownership of the software quality that we develop. So while you wait AI to finish your code, your judgement and critical thinking will still be valuable for the decision making.
